Welcome to the Disaster Relief App

We are a small team of developers who have been affected by natural Disasters and wanted to discover a new way to provide help during times of distress. When we are in a Natural disaster and all type of communications are down the last thing we want to be worried about is if rescuers will pass by your neighborhood or if they will figure out if you need special help. The Disaster Relief App was created to help the rescuers locate and give the help everyone one needs more efficient. In the next Step we will be asking only the most critical inforamtion that rescures need :

  • Address
  • Name
  • Age
  • Pets
  • Disability or Special Condition
  • Picture (optional)
